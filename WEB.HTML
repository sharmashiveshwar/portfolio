ABOUT ME

A bit about me.

I am a creator at the dynamic intersection of technology and design, driven by a passion for building beautiful, functional digital experiences. In my role as Tech Head for the Fakir Mohan Foundation, I bridge the gap between tradition and innovation—leading web development, shaping narratives through manuscript editing, and crafting compelling visual stories with graphic design.

My creative journey extends into the world of publishing, where I've designed intricate book layouts for Shubhi Publication and delivered bespoke branding and cover designs for international clients on Fiverr. This experience has honed my eye for detail and my ability to translate complex ideas into clean, impactful visuals.

Beyond design, I'm a dedicated problem-solver who enjoys bringing ideas to life through code. I build practical desktop applications with Python, demonstrating my commitment to creating tangible solutions. As an adaptable and versatile professional, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality work across both digital and print mediums, constantly seeking new challenges to expand my craft.
